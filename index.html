Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specfic amount of points to your score.

You WIN the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click the crystal.

Each time the game starts the values of the crystal gets changed.
